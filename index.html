Speakers

Liv Aasen – Designing for Sustainability

Liv Aasen is an award-winning UX designer focused on sustainable design practices. She has helped global brands incorporate eco-friendly strategies into their digital products. Bio: Liv leads initiatives on sustainable digital design and is passionate about reducing the carbon footprint of tech.

Sofia Dahl – Inclusive Design: Beyond Accessibility

Sofia Dahl is an advocate for inclusive design with a background in accessibility consulting. She collaborates with global NGOs to improve web accessibility for people with disabilities. Bio: Sofia promotes design that considers everyone, including marginalized communities, with a mission to make the web accessible to all.

Erik Lund – AI in User Experience

Erik Lund is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and its role in enhancing user experience. He currently works at NTNU in Trondheim, where he explores AI-driven design solutions. Bio: Erik’s work bridges cutting-edge AI with UX to create intuitive, data-driven interfaces that revolutionize user engagement.
